About Penfold

Bio

Formed in mid to late 2011, PENFOLD are an alternative rock / grunge band from Livingston in Scotland. Drawing on diverse influences such as Idlewild, Reuben, Biffy Clyro, Frightened Rabbit and The Xcerts, PENFOLD are a band full of big riffs, thumping drums and bass and strong vocals all pulling in the same direction. They are a band full of best friends with a passion for making their own brand of rock music dashed with some softer more musically delicate moments.

PENFOLD regularly gig around Scotland making trips the length and breadth of the country to play their music live and they wouldn't have it any other way. PENFOLD supported breakthrough British acts The Family Rain and Circa Waves towards the end of 2013 and are looking to make a splash this year with new EP 'Lungs' set for release on the 6th of June 2014 with the bands debut music video for lead track 'Sixth Sense'.
